# 📖 Project Overview

The Zomato Restaurant Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Excel-based Business Intelligence solution designed to analyze restaurant performance, customer

preferences, cuisine trends, ratings, online delivery adoption, and geographic distribution.

This dashboard transforms raw restaurant data into meaningful insights that help stakeholders identify growth opportunities, improve customer satisfaction, and make

data-driven business decisions.
<img width="1168" height="1" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a0a42845-fd46-4f53-9ad5-f1be3cf31aa0" />


# 🎯 Business Objectives

✅ Analyze restaurant distribution across countries and cities

✅ Identify top-performing cuisines

✅ Evaluate customer ratings and reviews

✅ Measure online delivery and table booking adoption

✅ Discover yearly, quarterly, and monthly restaurant trends

✅ Support strategic expansion and marketing decisions

# 💡 Business Recommendations

1.  🍽️ Improve Customer Ratings  – Support low-rated restaurants with quality and service improvement programs.

2. 🚚 Expand Online Delivery – Encourage more restaurants to offer delivery services to increase customer reach.

3. 📅 Boost Table Booking Usage – Promote reservation features for a better dining experience.

4. 🌮 Focus on Popular Cuisines – Expand North Indian, Chinese, and Fast Food offerings to match customer demand.

5. 🏙️ Strengthen High-Demand Cities – Invest more in New Delhi, Gurgaon, and Noida where restaurant concentration is highest.

6. ⭐ Promote Top-Rated Restaurants – Feature highly rated restaurants to drive customer trust and engagement.

7. 💰 Optimize Pricing & Offers – Use discounts, combo deals, and loyalty programs to attract more customers.

8. 📈 Leverage Customer Reviews – Analyze feedback regularly to improve restaurant performance and satisfaction.
